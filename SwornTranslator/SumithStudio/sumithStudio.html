Sumith Studio Document Accepting Forms

Please make sure that the people submitting the documents understand the purpose of asking for the following details and make sure that they do not try to answer questions that they do not know the answers to.

  • It is better to give the names that are in the passports.
  • But, to move to certain countries such as Israel there cannot be any differences in the names that appear in the original documents. So, the names cannot be slightly altered.
  • Do not fill in the form if the person submitting the document does not know how to spell a certain name. Leave that part blank.
  • Please make sure that you are sending the document to a third party to translate and that any whatsapp messages they receive are not from you.
  • Make sure that you are connected to the internet when you press the button send. The API needs an internet connection to send the email.
  • Please send a contact number of the client. It is essential. However, I do not plan to contact any clients unless there is a necessity.
